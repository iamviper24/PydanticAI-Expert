Getting Help
If you need help getting started with PydanticAI or with advanced usage, the following sources may be useful.
Slack
Join the
#pydantic-ai
channel in the
Pydantic Slack
to ask questions, get help, and chat about PydanticAI. There's also channels for Pydantic, Logfire, and FastUI.
If you're on a
Logfire
Pro plan, you can also get a dedicated private slack collab channel with us.
GitHub Issues
The
PydanticAI GitHub Issues
are a great place to ask questions and give us feedback.
